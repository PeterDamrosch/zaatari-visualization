Syrian Refugees in the Zaatari Refugee Camp, Jordan

Made for a course in data visualization, these charts show the number of Syrians living in Zaatari and the kind of housing they have, broken down by caravans, tents, or a combination of the two. The numbers are based on active registrations in UNHCR's database.
As large as the Zaatari refugee camp is, it still houses only a fraction of the country's refugees. Over 4/5 of Syrian refugees in Jordan live in cities. Since it is illegal to work as a refugee in Jordan, urban refugees rely upon scarce international aid. Consider donating to one of these organizations.
